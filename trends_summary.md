# Trends Summary — 2026-06-24

1. **Roguelike deckbuilders are the genre of the year.** Slay the Spire 2's $108M / 5.3M-copy run in three weeks of Early Access has reset expectations for what an indie deckbuilder can do.
2. **Cozy is colliding with strategy genres.** Cozy aesthetics are merging into deckbuilders and city-builders (*Islantiles*), and cozy gaming remains underserved on TikTok relative to competitive content.
3. **UGC/sandbox platforms still dwarf everything else.** Roblox's 144.5M DAU is larger than the next several entries combined.
4. **Mobile mass-market is stable, not growing.** Candy Crush / Free Fire / PUBG Mobile sustain huge DAU but represent mature, saturated categories rather than fresh opportunity.
5. **Interactive/decision-based livestream formats are the fastest-rising TikTok mechanic** — audience-voted actions inside a live game session.
6. **Indie distribution has shifted to TikTok**, now cited as the top discovery channel for small studios, rewarding distinctive art and unusual mechanics.
7. **Niche competitive genres (sports, racing, fighting) remain franchise-locked** — low near-term opportunity for new entrants without a major hook.
