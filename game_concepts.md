### "Settlement of Spires" — Cozy Roguelike Deckbuilder with Audience-Voted Runs

**Pitch:** A cozy roguelike deckbuilder (Slay the Spire 2 / Islantiles lineage) where each run also grows a small settlement, and — natively, not as a bolted-on stream overlay — a "town hall vote" mechanic lets a linked audience (TikTok/Discord) vote on key run decisions (which card to take, which building to place, fight vs. flee).

**Why now:**
- Rides the #1 confidence mechanic (roguelike deckbuilding) and cozy/systems hybrids.
- Fills market gaps (cozy + strategy, all-ages; purpose-built TikTok-native interactive mechanic) simultaneously — see `market_gaps.md`.
- Differentiates from Islantiles/Slay the Spire 2 by making the audience-vote loop a first-class feature rather than a streaming add-on.

**Core loop:** Run a deck-based roguelike dungeon crawl → earn resources → expand a cozy settlement between runs → optionally open "vote mode," where viewers pick the next card draft or building placement in real time.

**Risk:** Audience-vote mechanic is unproven as a core (not gimmick) feature — needs a single-player mode that's fully satisfying without an audience.

### 1. Safe Concept — "Tideglass" (idle aquarium collector + raid)

- **Core loop:** Grow and breed rare fish/coral in your aquarium; fish grow even while offline. Sell rare specimens, reinvest in tanks and breeding combos.
- **Progression:** Common → mythic rarity ladder; tank-size and breeding-slot upgrades.
- **Social mechanics:** Visit friends' aquariums; once per day you can "net" one unguarded fish from an undefended tank (mirrors the proven Steal-a-Brainrot/Grow-a-Garden-2 raid layer).
- **Monetization:** Gacha currency for rare fish, cosmetic tanks/decor, limited-edition seasonal fish drops.
- **Retention hooks:** Offline-growth check-in compulsion + raid loss-aversion (don't let your prize fish get netted) + daily login food bonus.
- **Emotional trigger:** Nurturing/collecting blended with loss-aversion.
- **Why now:** Directly reuses the two highest-confidence mechanics in `mechanics_confidence.md` (idle/offline progression: 8/10, steal/raid loss-aversion: 7/10) in an unclaimed visual theme (aquatic) instead of the saturated plant/creature theme.
- **Scores:** Viral 6 / Buildability 8 / Market Gap 6 / Retention 8 → **Avg 7.0**

### 2. Aggressive Concept — "Brainrot Olympics" (meme-creature live events + raid)

- **Core loop:** Collect absurd AI-generated meme creatures; periodic live timed "Olympic" mini-events (tug-of-war, races) where creatures compete for spectators; raid mechanic lets you steal unguarded creatures between events.
- **Progression:** Creatures gain power tied to real-world meme-virality scores — content pipeline must track trending memes and refresh creature roster.
- **Social mechanics:** Live concurrent events designed for mass-CCU spikes (modeled on Steal a Brainrot's 12.8M-CCU Bruno Mars concert) and clip-friendly absurd animation for TikTok.
- **Monetization:** Gacha for creatures, event tickets, cosmetic skins.
- **Retention hooks:** Live-event FOMO, raid loss-aversion, meme-relevance refresh keeps content evergreen rather than stale.
- **Emotional trigger:** Humor/absurdity + tribal status (whose creature wins) + FOMO.
- **Why now:** Tests whether "meme/absurdist humor as retention driver" (confidence 6/10, currently single-data-point) generalizes beyond Steal a Brainrot itself.
- **Risk:** Requires ongoing live-ops and a meme-trend-chasing content pipeline — high operational overhead, NOT realistically solo-buildable.
- **Scores:** Viral 9 / Buildability 5 / Market Gap 7 / Retention 7 → **Avg 7.0**

### 3. AI-Native Concept — "Hearth" (persistent AI agent runs your base)

- **Core loop:** Assign a persistent-memory AI agent with a personality to manage a village/farm/base. While you're offline it autonomously makes decisions (defend, trade, expand) based on goals you set; on return it narrates a story-log of what happened and asks for new directives.
- **Progression:** The agent's "trust/skill" grows as it learns your preferences — personalization deepens over time, raising emotional attachment and switching cost.
- **Social mechanics:** Share AI-curated highlight clips ("what my agent did today") — comedic/dramatic moments comparable to friends' agents.
- **Monetization:** Subscription for deeper agent behaviors/more compute; cosmetic agent personas.
- **Retention hooks:** Curiosity about what happened while away (asynchronous idle hook, stronger than plain idle growth because outcomes are non-deterministic and narrated) + genuine emotional bond with a "living" character that remembers you.
- **Emotional trigger:** Companionship + anticipation, similar to checking in on a trusted pet/employee.
- **Why now:** Directly targets the white-space flagged in `mechanics_confidence.md` — "AI agent as core gameplay loop" has strong macro market signal (4/10 confidence, lowest evidence but highest theoretical upside) and zero breakout consumer title yet.
- **Scores:** Viral 7 / Buildability 5 (LLM inference cost/latency is the main build risk) / Market Gap 9 / Retention 8 → **Avg 7.25**
- **Flag:** A scoped-down text-based MVP (simple village sim + single-LLM-call agent loop) is realistically buildable solo with AI coding tools within 90 days, even though the full vision is bigger.

### 4. Solo-Developer Concept — "Pocket Raid" (minimal-scope idle-collector, async raid)

- **Core loop:** Grow a small idle garden/shop around one core resource; async daily raid against other players' stored-state plots (no real-time multiplayer infra needed).
- **Progression:** Intentionally narrow — 4-5 rarity tiers, one upgrade tree, no breeding system.
- **Social mechanics:** Async leaderboard + a simple "send a friend a gift" mechanic.
- **Monetization:** Rewarded ads + one-time "remove ads / double growth speed" IAP.
- **Retention hooks:** Push notification when growth completes + daily raid cooldown.
- **Emotional trigger:** Nurturing + light loss-aversion, deliberately low cognitive load.
- **Why now:** Reuses the two highest-confidence mechanics (idle progression, raid loss-aversion) but trims scope hard enough for a single AI-assisted developer to actually ship.
- **Scores:** Viral 5 / Buildability 9 / Market Gap 5 / Retention 6 → **Avg 6.25**
- **Flag:** Most realistically solo-AI-buildable in under 90 days — simple stack (web/React + Firebase or equivalent BaaS), no novel tech risk.
