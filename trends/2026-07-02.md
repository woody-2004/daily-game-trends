## Key New Signals vs. June 24 Run

1. **Ambient/taskbar gaming with real-economy hook** — TBH: Task Bar Hero at 445K CCU validates demand for games that run alongside real work, especially with Steam Market income loop.
2. **Social camouflage/deception as viral clip factory** — MECCHA CHAMELEON is proof of concept: $5.99 solo dev game, pure TikTok/Twitch word-of-mouth, top Twitch category.
3. **Horror survival on UGC platforms** — 99 Nights in the Forest at 14.2M peak is a category unto itself.
4. **Narrative JRPG revival with active combat** — Clair Obscur Expedition 33's 8M copies and all-time Metacritic userscore confirm a hungry audience for premium story-driven RPGs when combat is modernized.
5. **Idle farming + asynchronous PvP layer** — Grow a Garden 2's night raids show the next evolution of idle farming: optional competitive tension without destroying the cozy core.
6. **Meme-to-game conversions** — Bongo Cat (97% positive, 150K+ CCU, free) shows internet memes remain a viable starting point for games if execution is tight.
