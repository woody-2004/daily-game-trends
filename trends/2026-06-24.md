# Trend Snapshot — 2026-06-24

## PC / Steam

- **Counter-Strike 2** — 935K+ concurrent players, still the top Steam title.
- **Slay the Spire 2** — Early Access since March 5, 2026; peaked 574K concurrent; 5.3M copies / $108M revenue by end of March — fastest-selling roguelike deckbuilder ever. ([source](https://www.demandsage.com/most-played-games-right-now/))
- **PUBG** — holding 200K+ concurrent in Steam's top 5. ([source](https://www.demandsage.com/most-played-games-right-now/))
- **Steam Next Fest (June 2026)** wrapped June 22 with 4,200+ demos, the largest edition ever. *Onimusha: Way of the Sword* was among the most-wishlisted demos (release Sept 25, 2026). ([source](https://tech-insider.org/steam-next-fest-june-2026/))

## Mobile

- **Roblox** — 144.5M daily active users globally, dominant cross-platform UGC platform. ([source](https://www.demandsage.com/most-played-games-right-now/))
- **Candy Crush, Free Fire, PUBG Mobile** — sustained mass-market mobile leaders; Free Fire at 13.95M DAU. ([source](https://www.demandsage.com/most-played-games-right-now/))

## TikTok / Short-form

- Cozy aesthetics, AI-driven content, horror reaction clips, retro revivals, and lore deep-dives are the dominant content categories. ([source](https://viryze.com/blog/tiktok-gaming-trends-2026))
- Cozy gaming audience on TikTok is large, highly engaged, and **underserved relative to competitive gaming** — lower competition for entrants. ([source](https://viryze.com/blog/tiktok-gaming-trends-2026))
- Indie games are having their best year on TikTok; the platform is now the top marketing channel for small studios. ([source](https://viryze.com/blog/tiktok-gaming-trends-2026))
- Fast-growing format: **decision-based livestream gaming** — viewers vote on in-game actions in real time. ([source](https://viryze.com/blog/tiktok-gaming-trends-2026))

## Genre / Mechanics

- Roguelike deckbuilders are cross-pollinating with cozy/city-builder mechanics (*Islantiles* — cozy roguelike deckbuilder + settlement building). ([source](https://rogueliker.com/new-roguelikes-and-roguelites-in-may-2026-the-monthly-update/))
- *Balatro* (poker + deckbuilding hybrid) remains a reference point for genre-blending success. ([source](https://topgamerarena.com/best-roguelike-deckbuilder-games-in-2026/))
- Niche genres (sports, racing, fighting) remain franchise-dominated with little room for smaller studios. ([source](https://www.superjumpmagazine.com/the-state-of-indie-games-in-2025-and-beyond-part-2/))
- Wellness/gamified health market projected at $3.8B in 2026; life-sim/farming titles (*Disney Dreamlight Valley*, *Coral Island*) drawing millions. ([source](https://www.blog.udonis.co/mobile-marketing/mobile-games/gaming-trends))
