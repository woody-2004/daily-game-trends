# Mechanics Confidence — 2026-06-24

Confidence = likelihood the mechanic keeps driving engagement/sales over the next 6–12 months, not just a momentary spike.

| Rank | Mechanic | Confidence | Evidence |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Roguelike deckbuilding | **High** | Slay the Spire 2 ($108M/5.3M copies in 3 weeks) and Balatro's sustained cultural relevance show repeatable, not one-off, demand. |
| 2 | Cozy + systems-game hybrids (city-builder, life-sim, deckbuilder crossovers) | **High** | Islantiles, Disney Dreamlight Valley, Coral Island all drawing millions; $3.8B wellness-gamification market in 2026. |
| 3 | Decision-based / audience-voted interactive livestream mechanics | **Medium-High** | Fastest-growing TikTok format cited across multiple 2026 trend reports, but largely unproven as a standalone monetizable product vs. a streaming feature. |
| 4 | UGC / sandbox creation tools | **High** | Roblox's 144.5M DAU is structural, not trend-driven — UGC platforms have held the top engagement slot for years. |
| 5 | Battle royale / extraction-lite on mobile | **Medium** | Free Fire/PUBG Mobile DAU is large but flat — mechanic is mature and saturated, not actively expanding. |

Lower-confidence watch items: AI-generated content layers in games (high hype, thin retention data so far); retro revival reskins (TikTok-driven nostalgia, unclear staying power).
